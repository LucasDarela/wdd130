Company Purpose & Values

Purpose: To inspire people of all ages to experience the thrill of nature through safe, unforgettable white-water adventures.

Mission: To deliver world-class rafting experiences by combining expert guides, top-tier safety standards, and a deep respect for river ecosystems.

Creed: We believe that every rapid teaches courage, every river inspires connection, and every journey transforms the spirit.

Motto: “Ride the River. Feel the Freedom.”

History

Founded in 1998 along the rugged river canyons of the Pacific Northwest, our company began with a single raft, two guides, and a passion for adventure. What started as small weekend trips for friends quickly grew into a full-service outfitter recognized for safety, professionalism, and unforgettable river experiences.
Over the past two decades, we’ve expanded our operations, trained hundreds of expert guides, and welcomed thousands of guests from around the world. Today, we remain committed to the same mission that started it all: sharing the thrill of whitewater rafting while respecting and protecting the rivers we love.
